About The Creator

I am a lacrosse player who plays for the Crean Lutheran Saints. I play attack and I also play midfield. This is my first every website

That I made by myself from scratch. I want people to use this website to learn lacrosse more and be able to build the understanding of the

sport. I would like to influence people to use this to build up everything about the sport at home, so they can go to a team/practice with

Knowing everything they need to know. I also would like it if coaches would use my workouts as daily warmups before drills.
